# How We Are Grid Consistency Correction

## Goal

Remove the distinct homepage image-panel pattern and make How We Are use the
same full-width premium card-grid rhythm as What We Do.

## Approved Design

- Remove the homepage team image from How We Are.
- Keep the shared chip, title, and description.
- Display six culture items in a three-column premium card grid.
- Match What We Do card radius, spacing, background, border, orange icon
  containers, hover feedback, and responsive breakpoints.
- Keep team imagery on the About page.
