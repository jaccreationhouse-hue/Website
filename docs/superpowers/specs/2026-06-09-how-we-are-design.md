# How We Are Section Design

## Goal

Bring the homepage How We Are section into the same visual system as the other
updated homepage sections without changing its content or team image.

## Approved Design

- Use the shared chip, title, and supporting-description hierarchy.
- Keep the existing team image.
- Present the image and culture content as matching premium panels.
- Convert the six culture points into compact cards with shared orange icon
  containers, borders, radii, hover feedback, and spacing.
- Use a two-column desktop layout and stacked mobile layout.
- Leave all other sections unchanged.
